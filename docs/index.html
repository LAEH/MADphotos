9,011
photographs, unedited

Shot over a decade on Leica rangefinders, a monochrome sensor with no Bayer filter, scanned analog film, and pocket action cameras. Most have never been seen by anyone.

The mission: treat every single image as if it deserves a curator, a critic, and an editor. Not batch processing — per-image intelligence. An AI studies the exposure, the composition, the mood, the color. It writes editing instructions unique to that frame. Then another AI executes them. From that improved base, style variants bloom.

Everything tracked in one database. Every signal extractable. Every image searchable by what it means, not just what it's named.

Three Apps

See. Show. State.

  • See
    The private power tool. A native macOS SwiftUI app for exploring, curating, and editing the collection. Browse by camera, vibe, time, aesthetic score. Keep or reject with a keystroke. Toggle between original and enhanced. Every decision flows back into the database. See is where the human decides what's worth showing.
  • Show
    The public experience. Three web viewing modes: La Grille (filterable photo grid), La Dérive (semantic drift via DINOv2, SigLIP, CLIP vectors), Les Couleurs (color space exploration). Only accepted images are shown.
  • State
    The system dashboard. Every pipeline, signal, and model in real-time. Progress bars, camera fleet statistics, enhancement metrics, vector store health. Plus the Journal de Bord, blind tests, mosaics, and vector drift exploration.
The Pipeline

Nine stages, per-image intelligence

  1. Render
    6-tier resolution pyramid per image (64px to 3840px), plus 4-tier for AI variants.
    render_pipeline.py
  2. Analyze
    Gemini 2.5 Pro structured analysis: vibes, exposure, composition, color grading, per-image edit instructions, rotation detection.
    photography_engine.py
  3. Pixel Metrics
    Luminance, white balance shift, noise levels, clipping, contrast ratio, color temperature.
    image_analysis.py
  4. Vectors
    Three embedding models (DINOv2, SigLIP, CLIP) into LanceDB for similarity search.
    vector_engine.py
  5. Signals
    EXIF metadata, dominant colors (K-means in LAB space), face detection (YuNet), object detection (YOLOv8n), perceptual hashes.
    signal_extraction.py
  6. Advanced Signals
    Aesthetic scoring, depth estimation, scene and style classification, OCR text detection, image captions, facial emotion recognition.
    advanced_signals.py
  7. Enhance
    Camera-aware per-image enhancement: white balance, exposure, shadows/highlights, contrast, saturation, sharpening. Each camera body gets its own tuning profile.
    enhance_engine.py
  8. Variants
    4 AI variants via Imagen 3: gemini_edit (per-image AI instructions), pro_edit (professional retouching), nano_feel (analog film), cartoon (cel-shading).
    imagen_engine.py
  9. Sync
    Upload all tiers to Google Cloud Storage. Track public URLs in the database.
    gcs_sync.py
